Applicant outcomes at a glance

This dashboard shows where applicants land when Socure models flag risk: fraud correctly caught, fraud missed, legitimate applicants sent to review, and legitimate applicants auto-approved. Adjust volume, fraud rate, and model settings below to explore a scenario.

Applicant confusion matrix

Each applicant falls into one of four buckets. Rows are who they actually are; columns are what Socure decided. The goal: maximize fraud caught (top-left) while keeping false alarms (bottom-left) manageable.

Fraud caught

Actual fraud flagged for review — the outcome you want.

Fraud missed

Actual fraud that passed through undetected.

False alarm

Legitimate applicant incorrectly sent to review.

Correctly approved

Legitimate applicant not flagged — friction-free onboarding.
